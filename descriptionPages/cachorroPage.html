Cachorro Do Mato Vinagre

The vinegaroon dog, also known as the bush dog, is a unique canid species found in Central and South America. It emits a vinegar-like odor when threatened. With a compact body and sociable nature, these dogs live in groups and cooperate in hunting. They primarily feed on small mammals, birds, reptiles, and fish. Due to habitat loss, conservation efforts are needed to protect this species.
